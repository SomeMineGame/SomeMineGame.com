What Is Invsee++?

Invsee++ allows for server admins to access another player's inventory and enderchest. They can add, remove, or move items with those in their own. This is great for servers that need inventory management and moderation.

How Do We Use Invsee++?

We use Invsee++ in two ways. The first way is with the classic buy and sell commands. It allows for items to be taken out to be manually tallied and destroyed. It also allows boughten items to be added to a player's inventory when they have space for it, instead of the give command throwing an item on the ground with a full inventory. Our second use for it is in legal court cases. If a player is ruled guilty of theft and is refusing to give the stolen items back, the judge can take them out of the thief's inventory and directly back into the victim's inventory.
