Command

&price amount item

Usage

Gives the price of the specified amount of the specified item. The price is multiplied by the inflation and tax rates.

Permission

Everyone

Example

&price 120 diamond

This will display the price of 2 stacks of diamonds along with the tax and inflation rates.

Enabled

Yes
