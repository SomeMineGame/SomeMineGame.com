Command

&buy amount item

Usage

Buys the specified amount of an item if the total amount is available in the shop. Random sellers are chosen until enough of the item is found.

Permission

Everyone

Example

&buy 128 diamond

If enough diamonds are being sold in the shop, a give command will trigger giving this player 2 stacks of diamonds.

Enabled

Yes
