Command

&voidBuy item

Usage

Buys a single item with money from your wallet that is given to you in Minecraft. Price is inflated by 25x as you are getting the item from "The Void".

Permission

Everyone

Example

&buy diamond

After removing the money from their wallet, a give command will trigger giving this player a diamond.
