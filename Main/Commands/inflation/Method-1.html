Command

&inflation

Usage

Get the current inflation rate. It works at a base of 100% (Normal) if everyone has $50,000. It'll go as low as 1% and gradually grow with a decreasing rate. Only factors in wallets, not bank accounts.

Permission

Everyone

Example

&inflation

If the average wallet size was $51,000, the inflation would be displayed around 101.16%

Enabled

Yes
