Command

classicBuy

Description

Sell up to 10 items at once at 50% the buy rate. These will manually be destroyed by an Economy Admin. This allows for things to be sold that the sell command doesn't support yet, or are very risky to sell. (Go here for a list of unsupported and risky items)

Permission

Everyone

Syntax

&classicSell [amount1] <item1> [amount2] [item2] [amount3] [item3] [amount4] [item4] [amount5] [item5] [amount6] [item6] [amount7] [item7] [amount8] [item8] [amount9] [item9] [amount10] [item10]
