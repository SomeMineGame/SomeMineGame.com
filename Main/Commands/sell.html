Command

sell

Description

Sell an item directly from Minecraft for 50% their buy value. WARNING, items sell from left to right in the hotbar, and top left to bottom right in the inventory! Make sure all items you want sold are towards the front of the order! Enchanted items removed WILL NOT be given back or recovered! Go here for tips and unsupported items.

Permission

Everyone

Syntax

&sell [amount] <item>

Possible Combinations (Click Row For More Information)

&sell item
&sell amount item
