Command

&buy amount1 item1 amount2 item2 amount3 item3 amount4 item4 amount5 item5

Usage

Buys multiple items with the specified amount. If any of the amounts are blank, it'll default to 1. The ticket will be created for the Economy Admins and you'll get a receipt for proof of purchase. Single word items are fine, but multi word items must be in double quotes. You'll be charged immediately.

Permission

Everyone

Example

&buy 5 diamond 15 "oak log" "enchanted book unbreakable" "flint and steel" bucket

This player will receive 5 diamonds, 15 oak logs, the custom unbreakable enchantment, a flint and steel, and a singular bucket. Of all things a single bucket.

Enabled

No
