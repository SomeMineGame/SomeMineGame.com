Tacos

Tacos are delicious! They are a traditional Mexican dish made with a corn or flour tortilla filled with various ingredients, such as meat, cheese, salsa, and vegetables.

Types of Tacos

  • Beef Tacos
  • Chicken Tacos
  • Fish Tacos
  • Vegan Tacos

How to Make Tacos

  1. Warm up the tortillas
  2. Cook the filling (meat or vegetables)
  3. Assemble the tacos with the filling and desired toppings
  4. Enjoy your tasty homemade tacos!

Whether you prefer soft or crunchy tacos, there's a taco for everyone!
