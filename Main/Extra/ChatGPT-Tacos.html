Tacos - The Best Food Ever!

What are Tacos?

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various delicious ingredients. They are a favorite food for people all around the world!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Protein (beef, chicken, pork, fish, shrimp, etc.)
  • Vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, onions, etc.)
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole

How to Assemble

  1. Heat the tortillas.
  2. Add your desired protein.
  3. Top it with vegetables, cheese, salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.
  4. Enjoy your delicious taco!

Taco Trivia

Tacos have been around for hundreds of years, with the first recorded evidence of tacos dating back to the 18th century in Mexico.

Types of Tacos

There are numerous types of tacos, such as:

  • Street Tacos
  • Fish Tacos
  • Vegetarian Tacos
  • Breakfast Tacos
  • Hard-shell Tacos

Taco Fun Fact

The world's largest taco weighed over 1.5 tons and was made in Mexicali, Mexico!
