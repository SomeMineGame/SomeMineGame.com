What Is Simple Voice Chat?

Simple Voice Chat is a plugin that allows you to talk to people as if it were real life by changing your volume and others' volume based on your distance to each other. You can only hear the people you are close to and they can only hear you if they are close enough. The further you go, the quieter you will get for each other.

How Do We Use Simple Voice Chat?

We use Simple Voice Chat to add a more immersive experience to the server. Adding the effect that you can only talk to people in voice if you're close enough adds some great detail to the server. Unfortunately, it is unavailable to Bedrock users, so they will have to type in chat or use a Discord voice channel.
