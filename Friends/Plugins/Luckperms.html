What Is Luckperms?

Luckperms is a permission management utility used to grant or deny players usage of certain commands or even features.

How Do We Use Luckperms?

We use Luckperms to grant players access to a default set of commands and features while blocking the rest. We also use it to remove access for certain players such as prisoners.
