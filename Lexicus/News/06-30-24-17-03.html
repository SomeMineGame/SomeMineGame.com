The SMG-SMP Revival

The SMG-SMP is once again seeing a revival on Tuesday, July 2nd! This time, SomeMineGame has decided to invite members of the public to join. If you're interested in joining this new server, head over to the Home page and fill out the application. Also feel free to roam the wiki so far to see what this server has to offer.
