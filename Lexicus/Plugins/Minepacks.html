What Is Minepacks?

Adds a linked physical and command backpack for users to use, with a size ranging from 1-6 rows. Admins have access to other players' backpacks.

How Do We Use Minepacks?

We give players all 6 rows of the backpack to use. If a player is a prisoner we revoke their access to their backpack. We only access other backpacks if required for reasons such as trying to crash the server or create chunk bans, although we'd ban them before accessing their backpack.
