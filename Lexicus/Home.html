Welcome to the Legacy of Lexicus Wiki!

We are a semi-roleplay server based around player run nations. We rely on our Lawbook for governance, legal procedures, nation development, and wars. All laws are voted on by players. We also feature a custom economic system with the use of a Discord bot. These are just the major features!

The word "Lexicus" can actually be broken into two parts - "Lex" which is Latin for law, and "cus" which is Latin for cause or reason. The "Legacy Of" in our name stands for the history and story our server will have told by the time it's done.

For a full list of features, check out the Discord Commands, Mods, and Datapacks pages!
