A Quick Quide to Enchantment Plus by Wyvernity

All images used on this page were created and are the property of Wyvernity.

Enchantment Plus is a datapack that adds custom enchantments to Minecraft, adds "crafting" recipes for all of the enchantments in this datapack and vanilla Minecraft, and increases the max enchant level.

To get the table and guide book, you need to trade with a wandering trader. The guide book costs 1 book and 1 emerald, and the table costs a diamond block and 1 emerald. Below will contain the contents of the book in case you can't afford one when you finally find a trader.

Sharpness and Power are included in Damage Related; Efficiency and Quick Charge are included in Haste Related; Fire Aspect and Flame are included in Fire Related; Knockback and Punch are included in Knockback Related; Looting, Luck Of The Sea, and Fortune are included in Luck Related
